**Meal Plan:**

# One-Day Meal Plan for Rock Climbing Performance

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete aiming to maintain lean muscle and sustained energy for rock climbing:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 2,400 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (110g), Fat (75g), Carbs (310g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Greek yogurt (200g, plain non-fat) with granola (50g), mixed berries (100g), and ground flaxseed (15g). Whole wheat toast (50g) with almond butter (20g).
    * Protein: 25g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 65g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Banana (120g) with peanut butter (25g) and a handful of almonds (20g).
    * Protein: 12g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 35g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Chickpea and vegetable curry made with chickpeas (150g cooked), spinach (100g), bell peppers (80g), onion (50g), and coconut milk (100ml). Brown rice (80g cooked).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 75g

* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Hummus (60g) with carrot sticks (100g), celery (80g), and whole grain crackers (30g).
    * Protein: 8g, Fat: 10g, Carbs: 30g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Baked tofu (180g) marinated with tamari and ginger, served with quinoa (80g cooked) and roasted broccoli (200g) with olive oil (10ml).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 14g, Carbs: 70g

* **Evening Snack (9:00 PM):** Protein smoothie with plant-based protein powder (25g), unsweetened almond milk (240ml), and half an avocado (60g).
    * Protein: 12g, Fat: 10g, Carbs: 15g

This meal plan prioritizes plant-based protein sources (legumes, tofu, Greek yogurt, nuts), complex carbohydrates, and healthy fats to support muscle recovery, sustained energy levels, and climbing performance while remaining budget-friendly.
